# Benchmarks

We have tested nebullvm on popular AI models and hardware from leading vendors.

- Hardware: M1 Pro, Intel Xeon, AMD EPYC and  NVIDIA T4
- AI Models: EfficientNet, Resnet, SqueezeNet, GPT2, BERT
